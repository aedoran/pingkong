Official Bitly Ping Pong Rules

  • Game to 21
  • Best 2 out of 3
  • Serve must be tossed atleast 6 inches in the air before being struck
  • Serve must be in line to pass through opposing baseline, if untouched (ie. Serve cannot be hit to opposing corner or tables)
  • Serve changes hands every 5 points – there is no “loser serves” when opponent has 20 points.
  • First service is decided by a “volley-for-serve” rally; loser serves first next game
  • Players switch sides after every game
  • Any other contact other than the paddle and table (ie. Wall / ceiling / chair / etc) is out and the point is over
  • Winner is responsible for adding score to website.
  • Any discrepancy in rules will be brought to 3 person panel (Gavin / Dan / Micha) which will determine the official outcome
